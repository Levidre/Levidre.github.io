Azura's update system


You can update Azura by pushing Ctrl+U or going in her right click menu if you're on SSP.
She would tell it when there is an update at the end of her booting dialogue.

Each update, Azura has a notice with all the changes brought by it. This notice opens after the update but is accessible in her Functions menu too, so you can always check it.


Last update


Azura is now able to hold Obsidian from the Zichqec's ghost Dusty and Obsidian. Some bugs are solved and it's now possible to add multiple songs at the same time in the music player.
