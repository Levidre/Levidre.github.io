About Updates

My ghosts' update system

You can update any ghost by pushing Ctrl+U or going in its right click menu if you're on SSP.
Azura would tell when there is an update at the end of her booting dialogue.

Each update, Azura and Zim and GIR are having a notice with all the changes brought by it. This notice opens after the update but is accessible in their Functions menu too, so you can always check it.

Keep in mind that I'm as regular in the updates as I am with Azurtale. So not consistent at all. There could be two small updates in the span of two weeks or one huge update after three months.
